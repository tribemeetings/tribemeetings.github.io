How does it work?

Advisor ⇄ Startup Dinners
in NYC Each Month

Startups and first time advisors, we encourage you to apply! (To be transparent, we accept ~31% of applicants.) 

Become a Member

Tribe is stacked community of technology industry professionals. We are here to broker meaningful relationships between first time advisors and early stage technology companies over home cooked meals (free of charge)

Monthly Invitations

Once you have joined the Tribe community, we will send one dinner invitation each month along with the location and guest list for the evening. We ask that guests bring one dish of their choosing (this is how we keep it free)

Advise or Invest

Eat, drink, and enjoy good company- that's really it! if there is a good fit, discuss next steps and let us know how it went!
